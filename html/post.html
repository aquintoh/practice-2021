Wedding

My husband and I met in a Mexican border school sponsored by the Church of Jesus Christ and the Latter-Day Saints. We both served in serve missions in Mexico Culiacan Mission. After we came back, we moved to Utah to get our education. We have lived here in Utah for 5 years.

We got married in April 22,2017. Since that day, we have had many blessings in our lives. I have attended the LDS Business College to get my Associate's Degree. After finishing my Associate's, I applied to BYU and got accepted as a Pre-Business Major.

Wedding day at the venue with bridesmaids

My Baby Boy

Before I started BYU, I had the blessing to receive my son Mateo into the world. Mateo was born on May 4th, 2021. He is 7 months old and it has been a challenge to raise Mateo and attend school at the same time. However, I know that my efforts will be blessed.

My Family

A great blessing that I have had during this challenging time is my family. They have traveled from Mexico to help me out with Mateo. I have been blessed with their constant visits.
